About us:

Welcome to Uncle Sammy, where the heart of America meets the flavors of Saint Louis. Nestled in the heart of this vibrant city, our restaurant is a celebration of all things American cuisine. With a rich history dating back to 1999, we've been serving up mouthwatering dishes inspired by the diverse culinary traditions of this great nation. Our chefs are passionate about crafting classic American favorites with a twist, using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible. From our signature St. Louis-style barbecue ribs to our delectable apple pie, every dish tells a story of tradition, innovation, and the warmth of a Southern welcome. We take pride in offering an authentic American dining experience in the heart of Saint Louis. Whether you're a local looking for a taste of home or a visitor eager to explore the flavors of America, we invite you to join us and savor the flavors that have made our restaurant a beloved fixture in the Saint Louis culinary scene.
